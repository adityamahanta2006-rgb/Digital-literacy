## 📋 Task 1: Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic
**Objective:** To create a high-impact, one-page visual resource that defines Digital Literacy and provides actionable advice for first-year B.Tech students.

### **Design Overview**
For this task, I designed a custom infographic titled **"The Digital Architect: Foundations for Engineers."** The visual is divided into three core pillars:
1.  **Definition of Digital Literacy:** Explaining it as the ability to find, evaluate, and communicate information through various digital platforms.
2.  **Essential Tool-Stack:** Highlighting GitHub (Collaboration), LinkedIn (Professional Branding), and Google Workspace (Productivity).
3.  **Digital Citizenship:** Quick-reference guides for professional email etiquette and two-factor authentication (2FA).

### **Technical Process**
* **Tool Used:** **Canva** (Manual Design).
* **Design Choices:** I utilized a minimalist grid layout with professional blue and grey tones to ensure readability. I chose vector icons to represent different digital tools, making the information accessible at a glance for my peers.

### **Reflection**
While creating this infographic, I found it particularly interesting to see how much "soft" digital skills—like email etiquette—impact one's professional reputation as much as technical skills. The most difficult part of the process was condensing a broad subject like "Safe Internet Practices" into three short, actionable bullet points without losing the core message. Using Canva manually allowed me full control over the visual hierarchy, ensuring that the most critical information (the definition and the tools) stood out immediately.
