### **Project Report (100-150 Words)**
A common scenario where poor digital communication causes issues is the "Incomplete Subject Line" when emailing faculty. For instance, a student might send an email titled "Help" without their name or reg no. In this situation, the professor may ignore the email as unprofessional or spam, resulting in the student missing a deadline. 

**Reflection:**
Clear digital communication is not just about politeness; it ensures that the message is received and acted upon. This task taught me that a well-structured email with a clear subject line is a vital skill for any professional engineer working in a global, digital-first environment.
