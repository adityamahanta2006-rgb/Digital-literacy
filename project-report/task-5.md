# Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness Case Study

### **Case Study: UPI/QR Code Fraud**
* **Target:** College students in India using secondary marketplaces.
* **Mechanism:** Attackers use "Receiver" QR codes to trick victims into entering their UPI PIN.
* **Prevention:** [Stay Safe Online Checklist](task-5-cybercrime/prevention-checklist.md).

### **Project Report (100-150 Words)**
While researching UPI fraud, I was most surprised by how attackers weaponize "urgency" to bypass a user's logical thinking. Even tech-savvy students can fall victim if they aren't aware that a QR code is only for sending money, not receiving it. 

**Reflection:**
As a result of this research, I have changed my approach to Two-Factor Authentication, moving to app-based authenticators. I have also committed to never entering my UPI PIN unless I am the one initiating a payment. Understanding these psychological triggers is essential for my role as a Digital Ambassador to help my peers stay vigilant.
