# Task 1: Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic

### **Objective**
To create a high-impact, one-page visual resource that defines Digital Literacy and provides actionable advice for first-year B.Tech students as part of my Student Digital Ambassador role.

### **Design Overview**
The infographic, titled **"The Digital Architect: Foundations for Engineers,"** focuses on three core pillars:
1.  **Digital Literacy Defined:** Navigating, evaluating, and creating information using digital technologies.
2.  **The Essential Tool-Stack:** Highlighting GitHub, LinkedIn, and Google Workspace.
3.  **Digital Citizenship:** Practical guides for professional email etiquette and two-factor authentication (2FA).

### **Project Report (150-200 Words)**
**Tool Used:** Canva (Manual Design)
**Design Elements:** I utilized a minimalist grid layout with professional blue and grey tones to ensure high readability. I chose specific vector icons to represent different digital tools, making the information accessible at a glance.

**Reflection:**
While creating this infographic, I found it particularly interesting to see how "soft" digital skills—like email etiquette—impact one's professional reputation just as much as technical skills. The most difficult part was condensing a broad subject like "Safe Internet Practices" into three short, actionable bullet points without losing the core message. Using Canva manually allowed me full control over the visual hierarchy, ensuring that the most critical information stood out immediately. This task helped me understand how to communicate complex technical concepts to a general peer audience effectively.
