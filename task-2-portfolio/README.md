### **02. Strategic Implementation**
I selected these platforms to provide a multidimensional view of my growth. **GitHub** allows me to demonstrate version control proficiency and transparency in my coding journey. **LinkedIn** was chosen to bridge the gap between academic milestones and industry opportunities, while **ResearchGate** ensures I stay aligned with peer-reviewed literature and scholarly trends.

---

Over the next four years, I plan to maintain a consistent "commit" history on **GitHub** to showcase my evolving codebase. I will leverage **LinkedIn** to secure internships and build a professional network early in my career. Finally, **ResearchGate** will be a vital resource for my final-year thesis, allowing me to document my academic contributions.
