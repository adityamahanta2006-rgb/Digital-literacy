* **Deliverables:** Concise formal email drafts (Extension Request & Internship Inquiry) and a [Social Media Checklist](./task-4-email-etiquette/social-media-checklist.md).
* **Scenario Analysis:** I analyzed the impact of poor communication, such as using informal "text-speak" or missing subject lines when emailing faculty, which can lead to missed deadlines and a lack of professional credibility.

## 🛡️ Module 5: Cybercrime Awareness & Prevention
**Objective:** To analyze a digital threat and provide a prevention framework for the student community.

* **Folder:** [`/task-5-cybercrime/`](./task-5-cybercrime/)
* **Case Study:** UPI/QR Code Fraud targeting college students in India.
* **Key Findings:** I researched how attackers weaponize "urgency" and "greed" to trick users into entering their UPI PIN under the false premise of receiving money.
* **Reflection:** This research led me to change my own habits. I have now transitioned to app-based Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) and committed to never entering my UPI PIN unless I am intentionally initiating a payment. Understanding these psychological triggers is essential for staying safe online.
